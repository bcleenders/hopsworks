2-Step Account Verification

BiobankCloud ensures strong security to protect your data. We provide you secure access to the BiobankCloud through 2-step verification:

  • In step 1, you enter your usual password


  • In step 2, you enter a one-time PIN code that is generated by the Goolge Authenticator app in your device

You are required to have a smartphone and install the Google Authenticator app. More guidelines about setting up a 2-step verification can be followed at Google Authenticator.

  • Downloading the app
  • Setup the account

In 2-step verification, there is a little risk your account becomes compromised and put your sensitive data at risk. For example, bad guys are not able to steal your passwords anymore and misuse your data in the BiobankCloud.



We care about security and privacy of your data, and that is why we require you to use 2-step verification.
